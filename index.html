My Story

I'm a passionate singer and acoustic guitar player based in Vancouver. Music has been the heartbeat of my life for as long as I can remember, and I'm thrilled to share my journey with you.

My performances are heartfelt journeys through my diverse repertoire of genres, including r&b, pop, and more. Whether I'm playing at intimate venues or private events, my goal is to create a memorable and immersive musical experience for every listener.

Beyond live performances, I'm dedicated to songwriting. Crafting original pieces that reflect my life and the world around me. Music is not just an art form for me; it's a way to connect with others and bring joy and inspiration to their lives.

With my diploma in Music Therapy, I have the ability to connect people mentally and physically to their goals and emotions through music.

Thank you for visiting my site. I invite you to explore my music, check out upcoming shows, and join me on this musical journey.

Your support means the world to me.
